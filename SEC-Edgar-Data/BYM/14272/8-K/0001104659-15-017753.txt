<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 5.02&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(b)&nbsp;(c)&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; On January&nbsp;23, 2015, Bristol-Myers Squibb Company (the &#147;Company&#148;) filed a Form&nbsp;8-K to disclose that on January&nbsp;20, 2015, the Company announced the retirement of James M. Cornelius from the Company&#146;s Board of Directors, the retirement of Lamberto Andreotti as Chief Executive Officer and his election as Chairman of the Board, the designation of Giovanni Caforio, M.D. as Chief Executive Officer and the election of Togo D. West,&nbsp;Jr. as Lead Independent Director, all effective as of May&nbsp;5, 2015.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:24.5pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On March&nbsp;2, 2015, the Board of Directors approved Dr.&nbsp;Caforio&#146;s new compensation package as Chief Executive Officer, effective May&nbsp;5, 2015, as follows:</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Dr.&nbsp;Caforio will receive an annual base salary of $1,400,000.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Dr.&nbsp;Caforio will be eligible to participate in the Company&#146;s Senior Executive Performance Incentive Plan or any successor&nbsp;annual bonus plan with a target bonus opportunity of 150% of his base salary based on the attainment of one or more pre-established corporate-wide performance goals.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Dr.&nbsp;Caforio will also be awarded long-term incentive awards valued at $5,234,894 which will be granted 60% in the form of performance share units ($3,140,936) and 40% as market share units ($2,093,958).&#160; The terms and conditions of the new awards will be substantially similar to the Company&#146;s standard form of award agreements under the Company&#146;s 2012 Stock Award and Incentive Plan.&#160; This is in addition to the annual long-term incentive awards that will be granted to Dr.&nbsp;Caforio on March&nbsp;10, 2015 as Chief Operating Officer.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Andreotti will become the Executive Chairman of the Board on May&nbsp;5, 2015 and he will remain an officer of the company for a transition period through August&nbsp;3, 2015 during which he will work closely with our new Chief Executive Officer.&#160; Mr.&nbsp;Andreotti&#146;s annual salary and bonus opportunity will remain unchanged during this transition period.&#160; Mr.&nbsp;Andreotti will not be granted an annual long-term incentive award on March&nbsp;10, 2015 nor will he receive any further long-term incentive awards in connection with his role as Executive Chairman.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On August&nbsp;3, 2015, Mr.&nbsp;Andreotti will become our Non-Executive Chairman.&#160; On March&nbsp;2, 2015, the Board approved his Non-Executive Chairman compensation package, effective August&nbsp;3, 2015, as follows:</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Andreotti will receive the standard compensation paid to all non-employee directors of the Company, including an annual retainer of $90,000 and an annual award of deferred share units valued at $160,000 on the date of grant.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Andreotti will receive an annual Non-Executive Chairman retainer of $200,000, paid quarterly, of which 50% will be paid in cash and 50% in shares of the Company&#146;s common stock.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
