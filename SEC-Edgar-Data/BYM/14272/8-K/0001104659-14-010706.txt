<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(e)</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On November&nbsp;13, 2013, Bristol-Myers Squibb Company (the &#147;Company&#148;) announced that Beatrice Cazala ceased to serve as an executive officer of the Company and that she was in the process of transitioning to a new role.&#160; As part of that transition, in January&nbsp;2014, the Company terminated Ms.&nbsp;Cazala&#146;s employment letter agreement, dated as of February&nbsp;11, 2011, covering her long-term U.S. assignment, resulting in the automatic reinstatement of her suspended French employment contract (the &#147;French Contract&#148;) with the Company&#146;s subsidiary Bristol-Myers Squibb SARL (&#147;BMS France&#148;).&#160; Ms.&nbsp;Cazala was offered a position in France which she has declined and, as a result, her French Contract is being terminated effective as of June&nbsp;9, 2014 (the &#147;Separation Date&#148;).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In connection with Ms.&nbsp;Cazala&#146;s departure from the Company, the Company and BMS France have entered into a Settlement Agreement and General Release (the &#147;Agreement&#148;) with Ms.&nbsp;Cazala, effective as of February&nbsp;14, 2014.&#160; Pursuant to the Agreement, Ms.&nbsp;Cazala will remain an active employee in the United States in the position of Executive Vice President through her Separation Date at her current salary and she will continue to be eligible to participate in all applicable benefit plans.&#160; She will remain eligible for her 2013 annual incentive award and the continued vesting of all previously awarded but unvested stock unit awards in accordance with the applicable plan documents.&#160; Ms.&nbsp;Cazala will not be eligible for 2014 annual or long-term incentive awards.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Ms.&nbsp;Cazala will receive the following benefits to which she is entitled consistent with her French Contract and all applicable benefit plans as an employee who is retirement eligible and involuntarily terminated without cause and who executes a general release:</font></p>
